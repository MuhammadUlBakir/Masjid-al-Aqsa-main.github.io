Copyright & Fair Usage Policy

Welcome to MasjidAlAqsa.com. Our mission is to educate users about the historical and religious significance of Masjid Al-Aqsa, and focusing on the rich history of Palestine. As a non-profit and educational website, we are committed to spreading knowledge truthfully and respectfully. Our mission extends to educating users about the harsh realities of the brutal Israeli occupation.

Non-Profit and Educational Nature

MasjidAlAqsa.com operates as a non-profit entity. Our primary aim is to provide educational content about the historic and religious aspects of Palestine and Masjid Al-Aqsa. We know that education is effective for fostering freedom.

Use of Copyrighted Material

In our pursuit to educate and inform, we occasionally use materials including but not limited to images, texts, and videos that are copyrighted. We do this under the principles of fair use (may fall under the the Copyright Act of 1976), which allows for use of copyrighted material without permission for purposes such as criticism, news reporting, and education.

Fair Use Considerations

Our use of copyrighted material is guided by the following considerations:

  • Purpose and Character: Our usage is non-commercial, educational, and aimed at providing historical and religious insights.
  • Nature of the Copyrighted Work: We prioritize the use of published, factual content and avoid using creative works unless absolutely necessary for educational purposes.
  • Amount and Substantiality: We use only the amount of copyrighted material that is necessary to achieve our educational objectives.
  • Effect on Market Value: Our use does not negatively impact the market value of the original work; rather, it is intended to encourage further exploration and understanding.

Donations and Revenue

Any donations received are used for charitable causes related to the people of Palestine and for the maintenance of MasjidAlAqsa.com. We are transparent about the allocation of these funds and ensure they align with our educational and non-profit goals.

Attribution and Respect for Copyright Holders

We strive to attribute content accurately and respect the rights of copyright holders. If you are a copyright holder and have concerns about your work being used on our site, contact us at copyright@masjidalaqsa.com. We are committed to addressing such concerns promptly and appropriately.

If you believe that your copyrighted material has been used in a way that constitutes a copyright infringement, or if you have any concerns about the content on this site, contact us at copyright@masjidalaqsa.com.
