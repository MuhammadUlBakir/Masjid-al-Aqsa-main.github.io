History of Keter

Keter is a renowned global brand specializing in resin-based consumer products for outdoor living and storage solutions. The company's history traces back to 1948 when it was established in Israel as a small workshop producing plastic household and garden products. Over the decades, Keter expanded its operations and product lines, becoming a leader in the industry. One of its breakthrough innovations was the introduction of the first plastic garden shed in the 1970s, revolutionizing outdoor storage solutions.
