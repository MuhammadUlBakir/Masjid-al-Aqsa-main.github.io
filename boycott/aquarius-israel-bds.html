History of Aquarius

Aquarius Water, founded in 1983, emerged as a pioneering force in the bottled water industry, emphasizing purity and sustainability. Their journey began with a commitment to source water from pristine natural springs, utilizing advanced filtration methods to ensure the highest quality product. Over the years, Aquarius expanded its reach globally, becoming synonymous with premium hydration and environmental stewardship. With a focus on innovation and community engagement, Aquarius continues to thrive as a leading brand in the realm of bottled water, symbolizing a blend of purity, health, and environmental consciousness.
