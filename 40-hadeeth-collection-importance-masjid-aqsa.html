Friends of Al-Aqsa

Prophet Muhammad ﷺ devoted considerable effort to educating his companions about the significance of Masjid al-Aqsa. He emphasized its religious importance and prepared them to embark on an expedition to liberate Al-Quds/Jerusalem which was under the control of Romans at that time.
